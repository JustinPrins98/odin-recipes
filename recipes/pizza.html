Thin-Crust Pizza Dough

This thin-crust pizza dough recipe is ready to bake in just 15 minutes with deliciously crisp results.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Flour
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Activate the yeast with sugar and water.
  2. Make the dough.
  3. Knead the dough
  4. Roll the dough into a circle.
